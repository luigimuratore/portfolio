If I had to describe myself with one word I would say: curious.
I’m a Mechatronics Engineer student at Politecnico di Torino with a knack for making machines move and robots think. I'm all about taking things apart (and ideally putting them back together) in the name of learning, from CNC machines to robotic arms. I've dabbled in everything from Design to 3D printing and Controllers, where I work my magic on robots. With a passion for innovative tech and user experience design, I'm diving deep into robotics, AI, AR, and VR, and I’m on the hunt for new adventure-packed experiences. Off-screen, you’ll find me grooving on my bass, channeling my love for the Red Hot Chili Peppers, or hitting the basketball court to clear my mind.

My motto? "Done is better than perfect."

My goal is to develop innovative solutions that address real-world challenges through the integration of robotics and automation technologies.
